SICP modeling data with functions:

(define (pair x y)
  (define (dispatch m)
    (cond ((= m 0) x)
          ((= m 1) y)
          (else (error "Argument not 0 or 1"))))
  dispatch)

(define (frst p) (p 0))

(define (snd p) (p 1))

pair is an object that responds to messages (‘0’ and ‘1’).

In this setup it is impossible to get the individual coordinates without sending a message. True encapsulation of state.

On writing well:

  • Choose a mode/tense/etc.. and stick with it.
  • How (in what capacity) to address the reader?
  • What do I want to say? Have I said it?
  • One point: reductions time/place. What to skip?
  • Think clearly => write clearly. Every sentence should build on the previous. Logical steps forward.
  • Don’t end with summary.
